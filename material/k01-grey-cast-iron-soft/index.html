ISO K — Cast Iron · K01

K01 — Grey cast iron (soft)

Hardness range: 180–220 HB. Part of the ISO K — Cast Iron group. Cast iron is abrasive and brittle — short chips, no built-up-edge. CVD alumina (Al₂O₃) coatings resist the high-temperature abrasion. Dry machining is acceptable for grey iron; coolant helps with nodular grades. Main failure mode: flank wear. High-speed machining is effective.
