Common Questions

How do I find the right tool for my ISO material group? expand_more

Use the AI Advisor to describe your application, material hardness, and machine type. ToolAdvisor will recommend compatible cutting tools across all brands, sorted by performance and cost.

Can I export my saved tool list? expand_more

Pro users can export their saved tools as PDF or CSV for integration with your procurement system. Standard users can view and print tools directly from their browser.

How often is the tool database updated? expand_more

We update our database monthly with new tool releases, discontinued products, and performance benchmarks. Pro subscribers receive immediate alerts when new tools relevant to their selections are added.

What does the Pro plan include? expand_more

Pro includes unlimited AI Advisor queries, advanced cross-reference reports, bulk export capabilities, priority support, and ISO material trend analysis. Full comparison available on our pricing page.
