PDF Extraction

Upload a manufacturer catalogue PDF. Claude extracts candidate products based on catalog type.

Catalog Type
Type A — Unified
Cutting data in same section as products. MAFord, Kennametal, Walter style.
Type B — Linked Pages
Products on separate pages, cutting data referenced by page number. Gühring, Sandvik style.
Claude will identify product families automatically and tag each record. Best for complete catalogues.

Type B: Claude will attempt to link product pages with their cutting data pages automatically.
