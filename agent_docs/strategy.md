# CuttingToolsAI Strategy

Last updated: 2026-06-11. This document is the single source of truth for direction. Every new feature, tool, or idea is checked against this before work starts.

## Vision

Become the decision engine of the European shop floor: the second screen a machinist opens when deciding on a cutting tool, a holder, or an abrasive. Brand-neutral, powered by field judgment (why-layer), giving answers generic AI cannot.

## North star metric

Weekly returning users (same user querying again within 7 days). Revenue is a lagging indicator; returning use is the leading one.

## Founder threshold

When CuttingToolsAI net income (after costs) is >= EUR 2,000/month for 3 consecutive months, Murat goes full-time. Checked on the 1st of every month.

## Phases (gated by metrics, NOT by calendar)

- **Phase 1 — Cutting tools (now):** single vertical, depth over breadth. Exit gate: 100 paying users AND weekly return rate >= 25%.
- **Phase 2 — Toolholding / collet systems:** starts only when Phase 1 gate is met. Expansion to existing users, not a new market.
- **Phase 3 — Abrasives / grinding wheels:** after Phase 2 is established. Also opens the B2B data product (anonymous demand intelligence reports for manufacturers).

## Revenue layers

- Pro subscription EUR 29/month (live path, launching July 2026)
- Shop license, 5-10 seats (end of Phase 1)
- Anonymous demand data reports for manufacturers (Phase 3, highest margin)

## 2026 milestones

- July: public Pro launch + first 10 paying users
- September: 50 paying users, recognized name on LinkedIn in NL/BE manufacturing
- December: 100 paying users, founder threshold in sight

## Never-do list (strategic arm of KIRILMAZ KURAL)

- No paid advertising. Channels: LinkedIn authority, SEO, word of mouth.
- No features without a metric. Every feature must map to return rate or conversion.
- No phase-skipping under calendar pressure. Escaping a hard vertical into a new one is forbidden.
- Never a catalog/store/SKU interface (KIRILMAZ KURAL).
- No spreading into markets or verticals we do not understand.

## Weekly rhythm

- Every Monday: research email + status session with Claude (what worked, what did not, single priority of the week).
- Every "gunaydin": strategy position + the one critical task of the day.
- Every new idea is checked against this document before execution.
