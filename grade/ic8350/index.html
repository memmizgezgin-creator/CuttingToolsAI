ISCAR IC8350

IC8350 is a ISCAR carbide insert grade (SUMO TEC® heavy roughing / SUMO TEC® PVD tough), applied in the ISO P area. In this cross-reference it appears on 2 insert designations with 6 comparable grades from Kennametal, Mitsubishi, Sandvik, Seco, Tungaloy, Walter. Equivalents share the insert geometry, not the process — verify on a sample part before switching.
