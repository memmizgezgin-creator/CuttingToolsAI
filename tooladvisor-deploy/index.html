Frequently Asked Questions
What happens after the 3 months at €19?
Your subscription automatically continues at €29/mo. You can cancel at any time before that — no penalties, no lock-in. The €19 price is locked for those first 3 months regardless of when you cancel.
What is Visual ID exactly?
Upload a photo of any cutting insert. Our AI analyses the geometry, shape, coating colour and size to identify the probable ISO code and grade — then instantly cross-references it across 7 major brands. Accuracy is ~80–90% on clean inserts.
Is the tool recommendation truly brand-neutral?
Yes. ToolAdvisor is not affiliated with any cutting tool manufacturer. Recommendations are based purely on technical fit — material, operation, conditions — not commercial relationships. Paid "Featured" listings in the Supplier directory are clearly marked.
Can I use it on mobile / at the machine?
Yes — ToolAdvisor is fully responsive and works on phones and tablets. Pro users can also use Visual ID to photograph an insert directly from the machine floor camera.
