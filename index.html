How ToolAdvisor Works
This is a cutting tools decision system. Recommendations combine operation, material, geometry, family and relation signals with brand-neutral ranking logic.
Signals used: material + operation fit, geometry and coating context, tool family/series consistency, cross-reference mapping quality, and relation depth (exact/functional/value).
Brand-neutral: brands are not hard-prioritized. Ranking is based on technical fit and comparable-signal strength.
Confidence score: indicates signal coverage quality, not guaranteed tool life. Higher means stronger evidence from mapping + comparable records.
Low confidence: appears when cross-ref mapping is weak, comparable products are limited, or profile signals are sparse.
Alternative types: Exact = closest substitute, Functional = can do the same job with some trade-off, Value = more general/flexible option.
